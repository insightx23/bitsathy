About us

InsightX is a national level symposium organized by the department of Artificial intelligence and data science in association with Kaggle & IAENG of Bannari Amman Institute of technology, Sathy. The main objective of our event is to promote innovative thinking and practical skills through real life technological projects. Different technical and non-technical events encourage students to rack their brains and brainstorm to provide solutions for any existing substantial problem. Our Tech fest is open to all. Students from various streams may come up with novel innovations and showcase them in this pragmatic platform.
